Creating wordcloud from books

Intro and Source of data

  1. This is a wordcloud calculator that shows the number of words that appears most frequently
  2. It currently have 3 books and they can be downloaded from

A Mid Summer Night's Dream:

http://www.gutenberg.org/cache/epub/2242/pg2242.txt

The Merchant of Venice:

http://www.gutenberg.org/cache/epub/2243/pg2243.txt

Romeo and Juliet:

http://www.gutenberg.org/cache/epub/1112/pg1112.txt

Function Step 1

  1. This is how you use it.
  2. You choose the book by selecting the dropdown box and click "Change"
  3. It allows you to select the frequency the words appear

Function Step 2

  1. It also allows you to select the size of your word cloud by limiting the maximum number of words in the wordcloud
